After meeting on Instagram, Dasha and I collaborated for the release of the first edition of her magazine, The Editorial QC. This spread featured makeup artist Corie Willet, better known as twistinbangs on Instagram. The images were created to show Corie's skillful hand of doing makeup on herself, as well as telling her story of her journey to becoming the public figure in makeup that she is today.
